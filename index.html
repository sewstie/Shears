Crafted with Tradition

Welcome to SHEAR, where tradition meets contemporary style. Our barbers are skilled artists dedicated to perfecting the craft of barbering.

Our Mission is to deliver a personalized grooming experience that reflects each client’s unique style. We believe a great haircut can transform not only your appearance but also your confidence.

services

Haircut

Customized haircut with an optional hot lather finish and a straight razor neck shave, ensuring a sharp and stylish look.

Starting at €40
Skin Fade

A clean fade from the skin up to the desired length on top, finished with hot lather and a neck shave. Perfect for a sleek, modern style.

Starting at €40
Beard Trim

Professional beard trimming using scissors or clippers to define and enhance your beard's shape. Includes conditioning treatment for a well-groomed and polished look.

Starting at €22
Long Cut

A haircut that starts below the neck or chin, suitable for maintaining long hair or transitioning to a shorter style. Ideal for significant changes.

Starting at €55
